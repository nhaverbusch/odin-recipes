World's most delicious rainbow fruit smoothie, yum!

Description:

Making a fruit smoothie with rainbow colors: now that's healthy and fun!

Ingredients:

Strawberry smoothie:
  • ½ frozen banana
  • 7 strawberries
  • 10 raspberries
Mango smoothie
  • ½ frozen banana
  • 5 frozen mango pieces
  • 1 peach
  • 1 raspberry
Pineapple smoothie
  • ½ frozen banana
  • 75 grams of pineapple
  • 1 peach
Spinach smoothie
  • ½ frozen banana
  • 2 handfuls spinach
  • 50 grams of pineapple
  • 1 apple, peeled
Blueberry smoothie
  • ½ frozen banana
  • 2 tablespoon blueberries

Steps:

  1. We will make 5 small smoothies in this recipe. Throw all the ingredients of 1 color in the blender and then stack the fruit smoothies on top of each other in a glass, so you get a rainbow smoothie. In between, we put the glasses in the freezer so the layers become nice and tight.
  2. Why do we use 3 frozen bananas for this smoothie? Frozen banana makes a smoothie wonderfully thick and creamy, almost milkshake-like!
  3. Start with the blueberry smoothie. Blend all the ingredients into a nice smoothie. Divide the purple mini smoothie between two long drink glasses and put the glasses in the freezer when you start making the next color.
  4. After the blueberry smoothie, make the spinach smoothie, then the pineapple smoothie, the mango smoothie and finally the strawberry smoothie. Is your smoothie still too thick? Pour in a little water or pineapple juice (if using canned pineapple).
  5. Serve the smoothie with a (reusable) straw.

Cheers!
