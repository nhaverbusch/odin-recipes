Rice with marinated chicken and beans


Description

Don't have much time or desire to cook? but feel like putting a delicious dish on the table? Then read the following dish! With this dish you will show your friends and family that a simple dish can be so tremendously delicious that they could prefer to eat with you every day!

This recipe is for 2 persons and only takes 20 minutes to prepare.

Ingredients:

  • 200 gr white rice
  • 300 gr chicken
  • 200 gr green beans
  • 200 gr mushrooms
  • 1 onion
  • 3 gloves of garlic
  • 1 vegetable broth cube
  • 2 teaspoon paprika powder
  • 2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 200 ml whipped cream
  • 1 tablespoon ketjap manis
  • 1 tablespoon soya sauce
  • pepper
  • salt
  • sesame seeds
  • olive oil

Steps:

  1. Cook the rice according to the description on the package.
  2. Wash the green beans. Cut off the stem end and cut the green beans into 2 or 3 pieces.
  3. Clean the mushrooms and cut into quarters. Peel and chop the onion and squeeze the garlic.
  4. Cook the green beans with 1 vegetable stock cube for 10 minutes and drain.
  5. Marinate the chicken breast strips with the paprika and onion powder.
  6. Heat some olive oil in a pan and saut√© the mushrooms with the onion and garlic. After 5 minutes, add the chicken breast strips and fry for 8 minutes.
  7. Add the whipping cream, soy sauce, soy sauce with 1 mushroom stock cube and bring to a boil. Boil the sauce gently until the chicken is done and the sauce is nice and thick and creamy (if the sauce is still very thin, you can thicken the sauce with a little flour). Do stir occasionally!
  8. Sprinkle with some sesame seeds.

Enjoy your meal!
